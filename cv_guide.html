Cover Letter Guidelines

Learn how to write an effective Cover Letter Application to Professors, Researchers and Scientists for a Research Internship.

When applying to a Professor, writing a proper Cover letter is of utmost importance. Surveys with multiple Professors has dispalyed that a poorly written, cold-emailed Cover letter turns them off even an otherwise impressive resume.

The time I spent as a research assistant at Stanford Center for Biomedical Informatics Research has helped me develop and fine-tune the skills necessary for a full-time research position. Specifically, my experience with crafting grant proposals has equipped me with the reading and writing skills you’re looking for in your ideal candidate.
Additionally, during my time at the UCLA Center for Molecular, Cell, and Developmental Biology, I learned how to manage my work in a collaborative environment. Together with 5 lab workers, I conducted thorough examinations of DNA and RNA structures, carefully documenting any changes or variations observed during the analysis process.
